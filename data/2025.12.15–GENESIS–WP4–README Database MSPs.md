## 1. Purpose & Audience
This workbook compiles structured information on projects contributing to the United Nations 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, and its  Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly in the domains of food (SDG 2), health (SDG 3), education (SDG 4), and water (SDG 6). It aims to identify and distinguish the initiatives being implemented by multistakeholder partnerships (MSPs). This dataset supports research in governance, law, development, and sustainability within and beyond the GENESIS Project.

Primary users: researchers, policy analysts, program managers, and students working on SDG-related governance and partnerships.

## 4. Workbook structure
- **Sheet 1 – Cover page**: Title, author, supervisor, affiliation, funding and disclaimer.
- **Sheet 2 – WP4 Database on MSPs**: One row per project.


### Columns in the main sheet
- *Project Number*
- *Project SDGs* (comma-separated SDG indices, e.g., `3,4,5,6`)
- *Project Name*
- *Source* (e.g., UN DESA SDG Actions Platform)
- *Status* (e.g., Checked – In/Out)
- *Type of Partnership* (e.g., MSP/Other)
- *Type of Project* (e.g., INI)
- *SDG Scope* (count of SDGs covered)
- *Description*
- *Start Date* / *End Date*
- *Partners* (organisations involved)
- *Info Link* / *Project Link* (URLs)

## 6. Data dictionary (quick reference)
- **Project SDGs**: List of SDG numbers (1–17) allegedly covered by each project.  
- **Status**: Validation state (e.g., “Checked – In” or “Checked – Out”).  
- **Type of Partnership**: Classification of collaboration structure.  
- **Type of Project**: Initiative classification (INI = Long-term Initiative; PR = Short-term Project; EV = Single Event).  
- **SDG Scope**: Number of the targeted SDG(s) (integer).  
- **Dates**: `DD Month YYYY` format (e.g., `01 January 2020`).


## 7. Using the dataset
- Use **filters** on *Status*, *Type of Partnership*, *Type of Project*, and *SDG Scope* to segment analyses.  
- Prefer the **CSV** export for statistical workflows (Python/R/Stata).  
- Follow the URLs in **Info Link** and **Project Link** for deeper documentation.  
- When reporting, include the **version** and **release date** from the cover page.


## 8. Known limitations
- Some **Description** cells are long-form narratives; consider text-wrapping and notes.  
- External links may become stale; update them periodically.  
- There may be **inconsistent SDG scopes** across sources; apply judgment when aggregating.
